## Overview

This project analyzes the global AI job market and salary trends for 2025, providing insights into:
- Salary distributions across different AI roles
- Geographic salary variations
- Experience level impact on compensation
- Industry trends and market demands
- Job market dynamics in the AI sector

## Dataset

**Source:** [Global AI Job Market & Salary Trends 2025](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bismasajjad/global-ai-job-market-and-salary-trends-2025)

The dataset contains comprehensive information about AI-related positions worldwide, including job titles, salaries, locations, experience levels, and other relevant employment factors.

## Analysis Objectives

1. Analyze salary and benefits distributions across different salary ranges and compensation scores
2. Examine the relationship between experience level and salary compensation
3. Identify top-paying industries and salary patterns by company size
4. Analyze work type distribution (on-site, hybrid, remote) and its impact on salary
5. Identify the most in-demand skills and their correlation with salary levels
6. Explore the relationship between years of experience and compensation
7. Build predictive models for salary estimation based on job characteristics
